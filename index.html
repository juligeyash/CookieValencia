Quick reminder about me

G'day, mates! I'm Cookie, a small and stunning feline with the most expressive green eyes you'll ever see.

Born and raised in Melbourne-Australia, I'm a proud 16-year-old Gen X cat.

My human parents absolutely adore me, and why wouldn't they? I'm a multilingual marvel, fluent in Cat, English, and Spanish.

I love soaking up the sun, snoozing, and finding the perfect hiding spots. I'm always up for trying a bit of human food and have a peculiar fondness for munching on cables.
While I'm quite social, it's always on my own terms. I'm a very chatty, tender, caring, and smart kitty.

The opportunity

My human parents recently got an exciting opportunity to move to the USA, and let me tell you, they were over the moon about it! However, they were also quite worried about my trip, but they never once thought about leaving me behind.

Instead, they did their homework and researched the best possible way to bring me along safely. Thanks to their dedication, I'll be joining them on this new adventure across the pond.

Preping for the trip

Continuing my tale, my mum really went above and beyond to prepare me for our big move across the ocean. She got me a super comfy travelling suit and took me around Melbourne with her whenever she ran errands. It was quite the adventure! She made sure to explain multiple times what was going to happen and reminded me to be brave and strong. For a bit, she even gave me some CBD to help with the plane ride, but oh boy, that stuff made me feel all kinds of crazy. In the end, she packed me a lunch box with my favourite treats, a sock stuffed with catnip to help me relax, one of her oldest jumpers for comfort, and, of course, Queenton my trusty teddy bear.
